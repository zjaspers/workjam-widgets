Quick Win
Manager Comments Within 2 Hours
Set expectation: managers comment (not just react) on all team posts within 2 hours. Model stores do this religiously—it signals "I see you, I'm engaged."
Culture Shift
Ask Open-Ended Questions in Posts
"What's one win from this week?" instead of "Great week, team!" Questions invite comments. Statements get reactions. Change the prompt, change the response.
Incentive
Public Recognition for Commenters
Weekly shoutout: "Shoutout to Jamie for the thoughtful comment on yesterday's safety post!" Celebrate the behavior you want to see more of.
Measurement
Track Comment Ratio as KPI
Add "Reaction:Comment Ratio" to your monthly engagement dashboard. Target: Move from 2.5:1 to 1.5:1 within 90 days. Measure progress weekly.
